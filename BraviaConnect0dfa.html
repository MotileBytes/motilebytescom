Bravia Connect

App-based Television Control for Windows 8 and Windows Phone

Download Bravia Connect for Window 8 and Windows Phone 8 and ditch your Sony remote.

With collapsible button groups and simplified QWERTY input, Bravia Connect is optimized for touch, with the most frequently used buttons placed at the outer edges.

Bravia Connect, paired with your Windows device, makes a great replacement for non-backlit Sony remotes, so remote controlling in the dark is a snap.
