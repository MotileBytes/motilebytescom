Deluge Connect

Remote Deluge Management for Windows 8

Manage your Deluge queue from your couch! Check out your downloads or snag the next download from any Windows 8 device.

Deluge Connect is a management client for Deluge Torrent, a popular BitTorrent client.

  • Manage your Deluge queue from any Windows 8 device.
  • Open magnet URIs or torrent files directly from Internet Explorer or Windows Explorer and have Deluge Connect send them to your Deluge instance.
  • Sort by Name, Queue Position, Status, or download ETA.
  • Add and manage multiple Deluge servers.

As a Deluge Web UI client, Deluge Connect requires a running instance of Deluge with the Web UI enabled:
